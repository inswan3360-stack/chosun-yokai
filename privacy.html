Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BLIPZONES. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and games.

2. Information We Collect

LocalStorage: Our games (Chosun Yokai, Flight Glitch) use localStorage on your browser to save your game progress, settings, and high scores. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Cookies: We may use basic cookies or browser storage to improve your user experience and for functional purposes.

3. Third-party Advertising and Cookies

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google AdSense & DoubleClick Cookie:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  • Google's use of the advertising cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

We also use cookies to analyze our traffic through tools like Google Analytics. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options.

4. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union (EU), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data, as well as the right to data portability and to object to certain processing activities.

While we do not collect personal identifiers on our own servers, our third-party partners (like Google AdSense) may process data for personalized advertising. You can manage your consent through the browser's cookie settings or via the Google Ad Settings link above.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the CCPA provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. This includes the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete that information, and the right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information (which may includes certain types of behavioral advertising).

To exercise your opt-out rights, please use the "Ad Settings" link provided in the Advertising section above or use a browser-level "Do Not Track" signal.

6. Your Rights and Data Control

Since your game data is stored locally on your device (via localStorage), you have full control over it. You can clear this information at any time by clearing your browser's cache or local storage. We do not store any of your personal gameplay data on our remote servers.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the practices of this site, please contact us through the contact page.
