About Us

"Preserving our ancestral legends through interactive digital storytelling."

Deep Mythology: The Core of Chosun Lore

Our archive meticulously follows the folklore development throughout the 500-year history of the Choro-era. We integrate records from traditional texts such as the Samguk Yusa and the Goryeo-sa, interpreting them into digital visual art. This ensures that every Yokai listed in our registry is not just a character, but an historical entity that defined the fears and hopes of our ancestors.

Historical Context: Why Chosun?

The Chosun Dynasty provided a unique period of cultural isolation and internal growth. This led to a very distinct brand of mythology compared to our neighbors. While Japanese Yokai or Chinese spirit-lore are widely known, the subtle, often philosophical nature of Korean ghosts and creatures offers something profoundly different. Our Registry aims to highlight these subtle differences, such as the playful nature of the Dokkaebi compared to more malicious entities.

The Cultural Significance

Mythology is more than just scary stories; it's a reflection of the social values and morality of a specific time. By preserving these mysteries, we are also preserving the collective psyche of our forebears. Each 'Rebirth' of a Yokai in our game represents a rediscovery of a lost cultural values in our modern, technological world.

Evolutionary Storytelling

In our primary game, Chosun Yokai: Rebirth, we've implemented an evolution system that mirrors the natural development of legends over time. A soul starts as a simple, undefined energy, much like a rumor. As it gains 'Divine Energy' (through interaction and knowledge), it takes shape, eventually becoming a fully-fledged Yokai that is recognized by the population. This mirror of sociological myth-building is the cornerstone of our interactive experience.

The Games

We feature two primary experiences:

  • Chosun Yokai: Rebirth - An evolution-based encyclopedia game where users collect and grow mythical creatures from souls into guardian spirits.
  • Flight Glitch - A high-intensity survival flight game featuring ancient sea monsters and mystical aerial biomes.
